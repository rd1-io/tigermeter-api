How to Flash

  1. Connect your ESP32 device via USB
  2. Click the appropriate flash button above
  3. Select the serial port when prompted
  4. Wait for the flashing to complete (~1 minute)
  5. Device will restart automatically

Requires Chrome, Edge, or Opera on desktop.
